Do I need a stylist?

Bend your fingers and count the answers:

  • I have a wardrobe full of clothes, but nothing to wear.For some reason, clothes don't want to go together. There's no chance of mixing and matching them into amazing and stylish capsules.
  • I want something, but I don't know what.I love shopping malls, but I do not like shopping. Furthermore, I just don't know what clothes to buy, how many, and where I can wear them later.
  • Nothing ever suits me.Yes, a lot of people around are trying to help, but they don't get me right. A friend offers me a red dress, but I don't like red. Mom tells me to be more feminine, but I have a different view of femininity.
  • I buy everything in a store.It seems to me that I can get on the right track. The term “impulsive buying” was coined specifically to describe my attitude toward shopping. My wardrobe is overflowing, but I repeat the same outfits over and over again.
  • I'm scared to buy new clothes.I don't like being disappointed by my decisions, but this emotion haunts me constantly during shopping.
